Hi! sounds.pink is developed by me, Brian Ellis. I am a composer, computer scientist, and experimental music maker. I love building things and making tools to allow deeper creative expression, and sounds.pink is here to share some of those things with you!

I originally bought this domain name to be a pink noise generator, but then I started building this tool, and thought the name was too good to not use.

If you need help, ask for it on github.
