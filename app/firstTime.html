Getting Started | stage | us

1.

Download the desktop virtual midi device.
Currently supporting Mac. Windows and Linux coming soon.

2.

Go to the stage and select your mode:

POSE is full body capture
EMOTION is facial emotion recognition
DEVICE is orientation and acceleration. Mobile only.

3.

Enter the code shown on the top of the stage into the desktop companion app.

4.

Get going! Midi should be streaming to your selected midi device.
This has been tested to work with Ableton Live and Max/MSP.
